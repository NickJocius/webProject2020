About Us


Beyond Suffed opened its doors in the summer of 2003 with our first location off the iconic South Lamar. Quickly becoming an Austin staple, we branched out in 2005 and added our second location servicing East Austin off 7th street and in 2007 we brought our famous stuffed menu to North Lamar.

Our head chef Nick Jocius was born and raised here in Austin, Tx. He has an unspoken kinship with the people of this city and tailors his recipes to give his own unique spin on this Tex-Mex favorite.

Our Mission


We believe in bringing our unique expertise and designer recipes to every dish we serve. Giving our customers and experience not soon to be forgotten. Our chef will take your taste buds on a vacation, combining the tastes of mexico, Italy, and the United States to utterly blow your mind and possibly burn your tongue.

Ensuring your meal will be unique, our main menu starts with your choice of pepper, next your choice of stuffing, then a choice from our sauce list and an option to make it a meal. We also feature inspired daily and seasonal special entrees to keep your tastebuds guessing.
